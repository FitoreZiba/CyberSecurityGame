# CyberSecurityGame

Cyber Detective Game
The app will be a Cybersecurity Learning Game, where users (students or anyone interested) can learn how to stay safe online through interactive challenges and real-life scenarios. Instead of just reading theory, users will play mini-games and solve cyber “cases” to understand threats like phishing, weak passwords, malware, and fake websites.
The app is designed like a Cyber Detective experience, where the user progresses through levels from beginner to expert, gaining points and unlocking new challenges. After each task, the user will also get clear explanations and tips, so they not only play but also learn.
We will also include an escape/missions mode, where users solve a full cybersecurity case step-by-step (for example: detecting how a system got hacked), making the app more interactive and realistic.

Different types of cybersecurity challenges:

Spot the Phishing
Users analyze emails and decide if they are safe, spam, or phishing attempts
Build a Strong Password
Users choose or construct strong passwords and learn best practices
Detect Malware
Users decide whether files, downloads, or actions are safe or harmful
Fake Website Checker
Users inspect URLs and website details to identify fake or dangerous sites
